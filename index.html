Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Jamundí.

Top three activities to do in Jamundí

s

Park Town

The Palacé Central Park has a magnificent statue in the center of it, the brave Cacique Xamundí who, according to Jamundí oral tradition, tells the story of a chief who, upon the arrival of the Spanish, resisted the invasion and hid a great treasure.

Town Mall

The Alfaguara Shopping Center is a shopping center located in Jamundí (in the neighborhood of the same name) and is the first of its kind that was opened in the southern town of the municipality.1

Beautiful Rivers

The Reserva de Río Claro rural subdivision is privileged as it is bordered by one of the most beautiful rivers in the department. The water wealth that characterizes Jamundí is one of the privileges that families who decide to invest in housing in this municipality can enjoy.
